About Devkisteel Maishamabati

Your trusted partner for quality steel doors, windows, and construction materials

Welcome to Devkisteel Maishamabati

Devkisteel Maishamabati is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality steel doors, windows, maishamabati (corrugated iron sheets), and other construction materials in Kenya.

With years of experience in the steel industry, we have built a reputation for delivering durable, reliable, and affordable steel products for both residential and commercial projects.

Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and timely delivery has made us the preferred choice for contractors, builders, and homeowners across the country.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable steel products that enhance security, durability, and aesthetics for homes and businesses across Kenya.

Our Vision

To be the leading manufacturer and supplier of steel construction materials in East Africa, recognized for quality, innovation, and customer service.
