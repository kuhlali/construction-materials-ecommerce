Our Manufacturing Process

From raw material to finished product

01

Material Selection

We start with high-quality steel sheets and raw materials sourced from trusted suppliers. All materials undergo rigorous testing for thickness, strength, and corrosion resistance.

02

Cutting & Shaping

Using CNC machines and hydraulic presses, we precisely cut and shape steel components to exact specifications. This ensures perfect fit and consistency across all products.

03

Welding & Assembly

Skilled welders assemble components using MIG and TIG welding techniques. Each weld is inspected for strength and durability to ensure long-lasting products.

04

Surface Treatment

Products undergo surface preparation including degreasing, sandblasting, and priming before powder coating. This ensures excellent paint adhesion and corrosion resistance.

05

Powder Coating

We use electrostatic powder coating for a durable, scratch-resistant finish. The powder is baked at high temperatures to create a hard, protective layer that won't chip or peel.

06

Quality Inspection

Every product undergoes multiple quality checks including dimensional accuracy, finish quality, and functional testing. Only products passing all inspections are packaged for delivery.
